## Design Readiness Rule

Before first-production validation, GPAO-T must prove:

- Korean is the default UI material.
- Work/chat remains the primary product surface.
- Control Center remains an inspector, not the whole product.
- Context, skill, model, authority, evidence, and replay states appear inline first and expand on demand.
- Approval and execution surfaces show icon, color, label, short explanation, and next safe action.
- Desktop and mobile screenshots show no clipped Korean text, awkward wrapping, or ambiguous authority state.
